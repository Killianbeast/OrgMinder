Schedule Events

OrgMinder allows you to easily schedule events, and makes them easily accessable so that your organization members are always in the know. Creating events is a breeze with our simple system, which allows moderators to create events on the fly with just the click of a button!

Manage Attendace

OrgMinder allows you to determine which events are mandatory and which aren't. Members can RSVP for events as well as send excuses for mandatory events. Once an excuse has been submitted they can be reviewed and accepted/denied by a moderator.

Help Your Members Stay In Touch

OrgMinder features a directory to store all of your Member's contact information so that members can always get in contact with other members when needed.
